About Us

The South Forsyth High Science Olympiad Team has remained in competition since 2011. From 18th at Regionals to 4th at State, these past few years have seen massive growth for the club and its members. South Forsyth has cemented itself as a Top 5 Georgia team, and remains a serious contender for National bids this upcoming season. Their motto, morior invictus, death before defeat, burns in the heart of every team member. Through their shared passion for science, competition, and pineapple pizza, veterans have become akin to family. Rain or shine, meetings are held every Wednesday in "The Aerie", their affectionate term for home base. Members test builds, take practice tests, and attend various event workshops. Students are expected to contribute time both in-school and at-home into improving at their respective events. The team generally participates in 6 to 7 invitationals every season, ending with Regionals, State, and the National Tournament. And yet, year after year, South has been denied a National bid, forced to end their competition season on a loss. They face tough competition, teams that have been nationally-ranked for decades before. But the members of South Forsyth struggle to take "No" for an answer. As the 2023 season ramps up, rumors of a "new era" have begun circulating on campus. The War Eagles are hungry for redemption, and they wish to prove themselves on the national stage.

QnA

Got any questions? Read the QnA's below to see if your question has been answered already! If not, you can submit a question using the form below the QnA's. You can also contact us through our email at southforsyth.scioly@gmail.com!

What is the process for team selection at tryouts?

Team selection is based on a short application, performance at tryouts, and, for veteran members only, past performance.

How much does it cost to join the team?

Joining the team costs $75, paid through MyPaymentsPlus at the beginning of the year once you are selected for the team. This one-time due covers everything from competitions to the team shirt.
